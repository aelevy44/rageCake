Shovels and Rope Count Down to "Swimmin' Time"

Husband and wife duo Shovels and Rope have had what could be called the summer of their career, leading up to the release of their second record, 'Swimmin Time,' later this month. This will be their first full album since 2008, when the two came together for what was supposed to be a one-time act. They spend the past few months hopping around the States headlining mega festivals like Austin City Limits and Bonnaroo, and joined fellow folk masters The Avett Brothers on the final day of a sold-out, three-day stint at Red Rocks Ampitheatre. This whirlwind tour has proven to be golden for 'Swimmin Time,' whose pre-release sales have ballooned with each performance. The record goes on sale officially on August 26. Preorder it here.
